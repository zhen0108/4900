Any Questions? Please e-mail  Prof. Lu


Program Fee, only $3,425:

  •       Round-trip int’l flights (NYC to Beijing & Shanghai to NYC; low airfare add-on for departures from other U.S. airports).
  •       Program-related transportation (including trains from Beijing to Xi'an & Xi'an to Nanjing) and excursions in China (Beijing, Xi'an, Nanjing, Suzhou & Shanghai), all arranged by professional travel agencies in China, with English-speaking tour guides and chartered buses
  •       Hotel rooms in all cities (double occupancy in nice hotels; single room for extra $750. We do not stay in college dorms whose conditions are far from enjoyable)
  •       Meals (see Note on Meals)
  •       CISI health/travel insurance (see Policy Brochure).
